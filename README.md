# EA Agentic Lab Stories

Interactive stories that show how AI agents solve real pain points in enterprise sales engineering. Each story starts with a familiar problem, walks through the cost of the status quo, and reveals how agentic AI changes the outcome.

These aren't feature demos. They're experiences designed to make you feel the problem before you see the solution.

## Stories

**01. The Onboarding Trap** - Your new SA won't be useful for 90 days. Here's what that costs.

**02. The Tribal Knowledge Trap** - "Ask John, he knows." John is on vacation.

**03. The Self-Learning System** - Your CRM learned nothing from 200 deals. What if it did?

**04. The Interface Gap** - You wrote perfect documentation. Half your team will never read it. Features an interactive "Choose Your Player" system where readers experience the story through their own cognitive style: Reader, Talker (video avatar), Visual, or Doer.

**05. The Question Tax** - Your senior SA answered "Where is the pricing doc?" 23 times this month.

**06. Starting from Zero** - 247 deals closed. Every new one still starts with a blank page.

**07. The Handoff Gap** - Sales closed the deal. Nobody told the CA what was promised.

**08. The RFP Fire Drill** - 200 pages. 10 days. Your team has done this 47 times, but starts from scratch every time.

**09. The Loss Nobody Learned From** - You lost a $420K deal last quarter. No postmortem. The same mistakes are happening again.

**10. The QBR Theater** - Last quarter's QBR was 47 slides of numbers nobody acted on. This quarter will be the same.

**11. The Reinvention Tax** - Your team solved this problem 14 times. The 15th time? Starting from scratch.

**12. The Snowflake Factory** - Every deal teaches. No deal transfers. Turning SA field experience into a living repository.

## Navigation

Each story supports keyboard navigation: `↓` / `PageDown` for the next section, `↑` / `PageUp` for the previous, `Home` and `End` to jump to the start or end.
